Become a Donor to Codename Canary

Help us end the scourge of pollution across the globe.

Codename Canary sponsors direct action to combat pollution in the environment. We fund specific projects that have the best chance of having a big impact. The resources environmental organisations have to put towards pollution cleanup are notoriously small, so it's important that we increase awareness, increase funds, and increase effectiveness.

To that end we are 100% transparent about where every dollar goes. Read more about our transparency.

Reasons to make a small donation:

  • You will help protect our land, oceans, and lives around the world from plastic, pollution and poison
  • You will become a part of the Codename Canary family, and be kept up to date with all our latest campaign developments
  • By donating, you're contributing towards passionate people fighting the good fight and changing the world one pollution event at a time!

Your donations will help us keep the website up and running, and help us build a platform to link changemakers with the pollution problems that most desperately need solving. As a not-for-profit organisation every dollar you donate to us goes towards the cause.
