How you can help

There are loads of ways you can help the cause.
Here's how to get started!

  • Join a local clean up event

    Get involved with a cleanup day in your local area. Registrations are open for Clean Up Australia Day 2016, but you'll be able to find something before then too.

  • Take 3 For the Sea

    Next time you're at the beach, make the effort to take three pieces of plastic home to recycle. Snaptrash is very proud to support Take 3. Got a bit more time on your hands? Try a 2 Minute Beach Clean and quadruple your haul!

  • Reuse Reduce Recycle

    If Jack Johnson sang it, it must be a good thing. Reduce your consumption of single use plastic items, like shopping bags and water bottles (ideally to zero!). Reuse and upcycle clothes and furnature - one man's trash is another man's treasure! And recycle everything you can! Most local governments in Australia have comprehensive recycling programs that should reduce the number of overflowing garbage bins we see.
