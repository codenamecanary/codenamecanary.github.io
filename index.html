What the heck is Codename Canary?!

A movement to keep the planet clean.


Codename Canary is a platform dedicated to getting anyone and everyone involved and invested in making the Earth a pollution free planet.

"The helplessness I feel when I'm walking along a beach that's covered in plastic bottles, fishing line, and cigarette butts inspired me to help the thousands of others who are also feeling helpless do something about it." Ben Plunkett, Codename Canary Founder

Codename Canary uses photos uploaded to Instagram to track pollution in the form of litter, garbage, trash and other general waste.

Through proud individuals around the world posting photos of trash on Instagram, we know where this pollution is coming from, where it's going, and where it ends up. From there we use this information to do something about it: by educating ignorant offenders, lobbying policymakers around the globe, and changing cultural attitudes towards disposing of waste products.

Here's how it works:

1. Snap a photo

When you spot pollution, whether it's trash, garbage, litter, or just general waste, grab a photo with your phone.

2. Upload to Instagram with #canarytrash

Choose a sweet filter, and upload the photo to Instagram with #canarytrash. For bonus points, include a load of hashtags to classify the trash, for example #cokebottle.

3. We'll see it

Your image appears on this map on the Codename Canary website, alongside the entire community's photos.

4. We'll do something about it!

All the data we analyse from the photos you upload helps us choose what the best course of action is.

From educating school kids in Indonesia on what happens to plastic that's thrown in the sea, to lobbying policymakers in the Phillipines to curb illegal dumping, your photos give us the data we need take action!

For example, if there's a large scale pollution event in a local park, we can contact the folks who'll come and clean it up. If there's litter washing up on a beach, we can trace where it's come from and cut it off at the source. With your help, we can start to reverse the trend of pollution in the natural environment!

Explore #canarytrash on Instagram

Thanks to all these photos, we're cleaning up the planet!

Got an image you'd like to see shared here?! Just upload your photo to Instagram, tag the location, and add the hashtag #canarytrash! Easy.
