We don't want pollution

It's dangerous and annoying.

We're a not-for-profit organisation inspiring the world to prevent pollution. Pollution is an issue affecting over 200 million lives aroud the globe. By being part of the Codename Canary community, you can help us make a change by raising awareness and improving those lives already affected by pollution. Here's how it works:

  • When you spot pollution, snap a picture on your phone.
  • Upload the photo to Instagram with #codenamecanary.
  • Your image appears here on the Codename Canary website alongside the entire community's photos.
  • We collate the data and use it to lobby changemakers to solve the global pollution issue.
