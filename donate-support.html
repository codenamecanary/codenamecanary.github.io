Snaptrash is a for-the-people organisation, so we want to make sure there are as many ways for you to support us as possible.

  • Upload your pics to Instagram

    Add your photos of trash, litter, waste and pollution to Instagram with the hashtag #canarytrash. For bonus points, tag your photos with hashtags that describe the pollution in the photo, e.g. #plasticbottle

  • Sign up to our mailing list

    By signing up to our mailing list you'll be the first to hear all of our new updates, and the first to be able to take action when we announce new initiatives and partners. You can drop your email address in the form at the bottom of this page!

  • Like us on Facebook

    Help us convert your audience to our audience. Like us on Facebook and help us by sharing our content with your friends and family.

  • Make a donation

    Every donation makes a difference! We currently use donations to fund projects run by our partner organisations, so every dollar we raise makes a big difference. You can make a donation online here.

Of course, you don't need to directly support us to benefit the cause. Here are some ways you can take direct action!
